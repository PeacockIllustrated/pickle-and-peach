Our Story:
The Dream

It started with a simple idea shared over a kitchen table: to create a space in South Shields where good coffee and great company could feel like a second home. We envisioned a warm, welcoming nook for our community.

Chapter 2:
The Perfect Spot

After months of searching, we found it. A little corner on Ocean Road with so much potential. It was rough around the edges, but we could see the light pouring through the windows and knew this was where Pickle & Peach belonged.

Chapter 3:
Crafting the Vibe

This was the fun part! Countless hours were spent choosing colours, sourcing furniture, and designing a menu that reflects our love for local, quality ingredients. Every detail has been chosen with care to create a cosy, modern feel.

Chapter 4:
Final Touches

The coffee machine is humming, the counters are polished, and the air is filled with anticipation. We're putting the final touches on our dream and are getting incredibly excited to finally open our doors and welcome you in.
